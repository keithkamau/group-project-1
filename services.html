Our Core Programs

Combining economic strategy with direct food assistance to build sustainable community resilience.

Economics

Cash-Based Transfers

Stimulating local markets by providing electronic vouchers and direct cash, giving families agency over their nutritional needs.

Health

Nutritional Support

Distributing fortified food baskets and bio-fortified crops specifically designed to combat malnutrition in children.

Policy

Social Protection

Strengthening national safety nets and insurance schemes to shield vulnerable households from economic shocks and crop failure.

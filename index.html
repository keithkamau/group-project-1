Welcome to FeedAChild

Our Mission

To end child hunger across the world by joining hands as one community. We bring life-saving relief in emergencies and use food assistance to build a pathway to peace, stability and prosperity for people recovering from conflict, disasters and the impacts of climate change.

Join Us in Making a Difference

Your support can help us provide meals to children in need. Together, we can create a world where no child goes to bed hungry.
